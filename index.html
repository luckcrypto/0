What is Luck.fyi?

Fair tools for lottery players — and the crypto-curious

Luck.fyi is a free suite of random number generators, odds explainers and live data tools for the world's biggest lotteries — Powerball, Mega Millions, EuroMillions, UK Lotto, EuroJackpot, SuperEnalotto, Oz Lotto and more. Every pick is generated in your browser using cryptographically-strong randomness, with nothing sent to a server and no sign-up required.

We don't sell tickets and we don't promise wins. What we offer is honesty: genuinely fair draws, plain-English explanations of the real odds, and the same cryptographic ideas — like SHA-256 — that secure Bitcoin. That's why you'll also find a live crypto market tracker and a BIP39 seed phrase generator here. The thread tying it together is verifiable fairness: tools you can trust because you can see how they work.

SHA-256 · powered by you

Cryptographically strong randomness

Most online generators rely on the browser's built-in Math.random(). It's quick, but it's not cryptographically strong — the sequence can be predictable if someone guesses the starting seed.

For every major lottery we support, we also offer a more secure version we call our SHA-256 generator. Before your first numbers appear, you move your finger or mouse inside a canvas. We collect hundreds of unpredictable movements, turn them into one string, and pass it through SHA-256 — the same hashing algorithm used by Bitcoin and secure passwords.

The result is a high-quality, one-time cryptographic seed. That makes each line much harder to predict or recreate, even by someone who knows the exact moment you opened the page. Note: stronger entropy does not change your odds — every combination always has exactly the same chance. It simply means your picks aren't the product of weak, repeatable randomness.

How it works

From a tap to a fair line

Secure randomness

We call crypto.getRandomValues — the browser's cryptographically-secure generator — and use rejection sampling so no number is even slightly favoured.

Real game rules

Each generator uses the official format for that game: the correct main-number range and the right special ball (Powerball, Mega Ball, Lucky Stars), with duplicates removed.

Verifiable & private

The line is drawn and displayed on your device only. Want more? Our SHA-256 "entropy" generators let your own mouse movement seed the draw.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are the lottery number generators really random?
Yes. They use your device's cryptographically-secure RNG (crypto.getRandomValues) with rejection sampling to avoid bias, so every valid combination is equally likely — and nothing is sent to a server.
Does using a generator improve my chances of winning?
No. Lotteries are pure chance and every combination always has the same probability. A generator removes human bias and saves time — it cannot beat the odds, and no tool can.
Is Luck.fyi free? Do I need an account?
It's completely free with no sign-up. Every tool runs entirely in your browser.
Which lotteries are supported?
Powerball, Mega Millions, EuroMillions, UK Lotto, EuroJackpot, SuperEnalotto, Oz Lotto and more, each with a dedicated generator and odds page. See all generators.
What is the SHA-256 (entropy) generator?
An optional mode where your own mouse or finger movement is hashed with SHA-256 to seed the draw — extra-strong, one-time randomness. It doesn't change your odds, only the quality of the randomness.
Do you store the numbers I generate?
No. Generation happens locally and privately; nothing is logged or saved by us.
Why does a lottery site also have crypto tools?
They share a foundation: SHA-256, the hash that secures Bitcoin, also powers our entropy generators. So we include a live crypto tracker and a BIP39 seed phrase tool — same principle of verifiable fairness.

Understanding generators & your chances of winning

Lottery number generators — standard and SHA-256 alike — only create random combinations for you to play. They do not predict future draws, analyse past results for an edge, or use any secret algorithm to improve your odds.

Every official draw is independently random and regulated. Each possible combination has exactly the same probability of being drawn, no matter how the numbers were chosen. Lotteries are games of pure chance — no strategy, pattern, or hot/cold analysis can increase your chance of winning.
